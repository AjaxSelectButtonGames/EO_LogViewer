Endless-Online Log View

Make sure to change the location of your CHATLOG.txt in your config.ini within the logviewer folder. You can also just place logviewer in the EO folder. 

The tabs 

[ALL] Shows all messages coming into your chat file 

[GLOBAL] shows global/guild messages coming into your CHATLOG

[SYSTEM] shows all system messages coming from EO such as exp it also has a total exp on the screen. 

If you double click on a chat line in the all tabs you can see all messages that came from that user in your chatlogs.
